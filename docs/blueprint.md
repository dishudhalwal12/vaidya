# **App Name**: CliniSight

## Core Features:

- Smart Notes with AI Drafting: Use AI to auto-summarize patient data and suggest assessment and plan options for progress notes, using a tool to know when and whether to incorporate information.
- Clinical Decision Support: Provide non-blocking and blocking alerts for safety issues with explanations based on clinical guidelines, using a tool to make the correct decision about which information to provide the user.
- Patient Portal: Enable patients to view appointments, messages, care plans, and vitals.
- Task & Care Plan Manager: Manage care plans with role assignments and automated escalations using a Kanban and timeline hybrid.
- Claims & Coding Assistant: Assist coders by mapping visit notes to ICD/CPT codes, providing confidence scores and change history, using a tool to perform clinical NLP
- Admin Console: Configure roles, permissions, content templates, and AI governance settings.
- Telehealth Integration: Provide HIPAA-secure video consultations with pre-visit checklists and document sharing.
- AI Attendance Tracker: Automatic staff check-in/out via geofenced mobile app or clinic Wi-Fi presence.
- Shift & Roster Automation: Auto-generate weekly schedules based on past patient volume and doctor availability.
- Performance & Payroll AI: Automatically calculate worked hours, leaves, bonuses, and commissions (for receptionists, nurses, technicians). AI insights provided
- WhatsApp API Integration: Send automated yet personalized messages to regular patients
- AI Health Campaign Manager: Build and launch custom marketing campaigns targeted towards patients by age, chronic conditions, last visit date, and risk level.
- Review & Reputation Automation: After each visit, AI sends a review request link via WhatsApp or email.
- AI Reception Assistant: Virtual front desk chatbot handles appointment booking/rescheduling, payment and billing inquiries, directions, clinic hours, and document uploads.
- Auto Check-In & Queue System: QR-based check-in at clinic entrance or kiosk. Patient receives a digital token and real-time queue updates via WhatsApp or in-app notifications
- Walk-In Optimization: Detects walk-ins vs booked patients via phone number recognition. AI dynamically adjusts the schedule
- Smart Billing & Payments: AI auto-generates invoices after consultation closure (linked to services used).
- Expense & Inventory Manager: Tracks medicine, consumables, and device stock levels.
- Profit & Performance Dashboard: Displays real-time clinic financial health: Daily revenue, average consultation fee, cost breakdown, no-show rate, patient churn.
- Voice-Controlled Daily Summary: Morning digest using voice through web or mobile (Gemini speech interface).
- Smart To-Do & Delegation System: Auto-creates tasks from patient notes and routes them to staff or virtual assistant queues.
- AI Practice Insights: Daily/weekly report generated automatically regarding Patient trends, treatment outcomes, marketing performance, and top revenue sources.
- Doctor Referral Network: Build trusted network with other local doctors. Send/receive patient referrals securely (FHIR data packets).
- Local SEO & Discovery Engine: Auto-updates doctorâ€™s clinic hours, reviews, and top services to Google My Business, Practo, Zocdoc, or regional equivalents.
- Membership / Subscription Programs: Enable recurring patient plans (e.g., monthly diabetes check, wellness membership).
- Clinic Automation Dashboard: Single view for all operational metrics: staff status, patient queue, payments, marketing campaigns.
- AI Alert Feed: Smart notifications based on priority and impact
- Workflow Builder: Visual builder for clinic logic
- Local Storage Integration: Utilize local storage to persist app data across browser refreshes, ensuring data is not lost on refresh.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Black (#000000) for a clean, clinical, and professional feel.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) to maintain low contrast and high information density for clinicians.
- Accent color: Red (#FF0000) for critical alerts and important highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for clear, modern interface and high legibility in dense clinical contexts
- Use clear, clinical icons sourced from a standard library like Font Awesome or Material Icons.
- Maintain an 8pt layout grid with role-first UX for clinicians, nurses, care coordinators, billing specialists, patients, and IT admins.
- Use subtle micro-interactions, such as predictive text shimmer and inline loading skeletons, to provide unobtrusive feedback.
